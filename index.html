ABOUT US

SpAIce was created by Eduardo Pertierra and Irene Granados, two space enthusiasts who wanted to make the cosmos accessible to everyone. We believe exploring real scientific data should be beautiful, interactive, and fun.

This project was born at the Hamburg AI Hackathon and has grown into a full 3D exploration platform powered by NASA's Exoplanet Archive and Google Gemini AI.
