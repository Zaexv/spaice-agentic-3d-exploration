What is this game?
SpAIce is a 3D space exploration experience built on real NASA exoplanet data. Fly your ship through the catalog, click planets to learn their details, and use the AI companion to narrate what you discover.
What you can do
  • Explore thousands of real exoplanets in 3D.
  • Open a planet’s detail panel and read its NASA-based stats.
  • Use the Planet Selector to quickly jump to interesting worlds.
  • Talk to SpAIce (AI) for narration and questions about planets.
  • Switch camera view (cockpit / chase) for different flight feel.

Quick tips
  • Use T to find planets fast, then click to inspect them.
  • If the screen feels busy, press H to hide/show UI.
  • Try V to switch to cockpit view for immersion.
